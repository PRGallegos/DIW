We Are Punk

Sum 41 is a Canadian rock band formed in 1996 in Ajax, Ontario. The current lineup includes Deryck Whibley (vocals and guitar), Dave Baksh (guitar), Jason McCaslin (bass), Tom Thacker (guitar and keyboards), and Frank Zummo (drums). They have sold over 10 million albums, with hits like All Killer No Filler (2001) and their single "Fat Lip," which reached number one on the Billboard chart. Throughout their career, they have released eight albums and received several nominations and awards, standing out in the rock scene with tracks like "Blood in My Eyes."

Awards

Juno Awards (Canada)

2003: Won the award for Group of the Year. 2005: Won the award for Rock Album of the Year for Chuck.

Kerrang! Awards (United Kingdom)

2002: Won the award for Best Live Band. 2004: Won the award for Best International Artist.

Alternative Press Music Awards (United States)

2017: Won the award for Comeback Album of the Year for 13 Voices.

Nominations

Grammy Awards: Nominated for Best Hard Rock/Metal Performance for the song "Blood in My Eyes" in 2012.

MTV Video Music Awards (VMA)

Received multiple nominations, including Best Rock Video and Best International Video for their popular singles.

MuchMusic Video Awards (Canada)

Received numerous nominations in various categories over the years, such as Best Group Video and Viewer's Choice Favorite Video.
