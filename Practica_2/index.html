Our Story

We Are Punk

Sum 41 is a Canadian rock band formed in 1996 in Ajax, Ontario. The current lineup includes Deryck Whibley (vocals and guitar), Dave Baksh (guitar), Jason McCaslin (bass), Tom Thacker (guitar and keyboards), and Frank Zummo (drums).

They have sold over 10 million albums, with hits like All Killer No Filler (2001) and their single "Fat Lip," which reached number one on the Billboard chart. Throughout their career, they have released eight albums and received several nominations and awards, standing out in the rock scene with tracks like "Blood in My Eyes."
