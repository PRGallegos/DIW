Meet the Drivers

Formula 1 is as much about precision engineering as it is about the skill, bravery, and resilience of the drivers who push the limits on the track.
Each driver brings unique talent, personality, and a relentless pursuit of victory. From seasoned world champions to rising stars, discover the individuals who fuel the heart of F1. Here, you’ll get insights into their achievements, racecraft, and lives beyondthe circuits.

Fernando Alonso

Achievements: 2-time World Champion, one of the most versatile drivers in motorsport.

Highlights: Known for his racecraft and adaptability, Alonso is revered as one of F1's all-time greats.

Off-track: Competes in endurance racing and runs his own karting school for young drivers.

Max Verstappen

Achievements: 3-time World Champion, youngest race winner in F1 history.

Highlights: Renowned for his bold overtakes and exceptional car control, Verstappen is seen as a rising legend.

Off-track: Enjoys sim racing and working with Red Bull on various promotional events.

Lewis Hamilton

Achievements: 7-time World Champion, holds records for most pole positions and race wins.

Highlights: Known for his aggressive driving style, Hamilton is one of the most skilled and strategic drivers on the grid.

Off-track: Involved in fashion design, music, and advocates for social justice and environmental issues.

Charles Leclerc

Achievements: Multiple race wins, Ferrari’s first Monegasque driver.

Highlights: Known for his precision and adaptability, Leclerc brings talent and resilience to the Ferrari team.

Off-track: Active in charitable causes and a regular participant in sim racing competitions.

Sergio Pérez

Achievements: Multiple podium finishes, winner of the 2020 Sakhir GP.

Highlights: Known for his tire management and defensive driving, Pérez is a key player for Red Bull.

Off-track: Involved in charity work in Mexico, supporting children and families in need.

Carlos Sainz Jr.

Achievements: Consistent points finisher, multiple podiums with Ferrari.

Highlights: Known for his strategic approach, Sainz has built a strong reputation as a reliable and skilled driver.

Off-track: Enjoys golf and has his own karting academy in Spain.

George Russell

Achievements: Formula 2 champion, first F1 victory in 2022.

Highlights: Known for his precision and consistency, Russell is a rising talent at Mercedes.

Off-track: Supports charities focused on children's education and mental health.

Esteban Ocon

Achievements: Winner of the 2021 Hungarian GP.

Highlights: Known for his resilience and determination, Ocon has proven himself as a formidable competitor.

Off-track: Active in promoting STEM education for underprivileged youth.

Valtteri Bottas

Achievements: Multiple race wins and podium finishes with Mercedes.

Highlights: Bottas is known for his teamwork and reliability, making him a valuable asset to Alfa Romeo.

Off-track: Enjoys cycling and has invested in coffee production.

Lando Norris

Achievements: Multiple podium finishes with McLaren.

Highlights: Known for his humor and talent, Norris has quickly become a fan favorite in F1.

Off-track: Runs his own esports team and engages with fans through social media.

Pierre Gasly

Achievements: Winner of the 2020 Italian GP.

Highlights: Known for his perseverance, Gasly has proven his resilience and skill on the track.

Off-track: Enjoys fitness training and raises awareness for mental health causes.

Yuki Tsunoda

Achievements: First Japanese driver on the grid in nearly a decade.

Highlights: Known for his agility and quick reflexes, Tsunoda brings youthful energy to AlphaTauri.

Off-track: Passionate about cooking and enjoys exploring new cuisines.

Kevin Magnussen

Achievements: Numerous points finishes with Haas.

Highlights: Known for his no-nonsense approach, Magnussen is a tough competitor on the track.

Off-track: Enjoys spending time with family and advocates for sustainable motorsport.

Nico Hülkenberg

Achievements: Le Mans 24 Hours winner.

Highlights: Known for his racecraft and consistency, Hülkenberg brings experience and skill.

Off-track: Invests in real estate and mentors young drivers.

Alexander Albon

Achievements: First Thai driver to score points in F1.

Highlights: Known for his comeback spirit and adaptability, Albon continues to show resilience.

Off-track: Active in animal welfare and environmental conservation efforts.
